📐 KELLY CRITERION — BET SIZING
Kelly tells you the optimal % of your bankroll to wager based on your edge. Bigger edge = bigger bet. We use fractional Kelly (½ by default) because our models aren't perfect.
Formula: Kelly % = (model_prob − market_price) / (1 − market_price)
Half Kelly: Bet half the recommended amount for safety
Correlation penalty: If you bet the same game twice (ML + prop), Kelly auto-reduces
Confidence weight: Both models agree → higher Kelly. Disagree → quarter Kelly
Max exposure: Never more than 25% of bankroll across all bets tonight
